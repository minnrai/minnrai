How to Download YouTube Videos

  1. Copy the YouTube video URL from your browser
  2. Paste the URL in the input field above
  3. Choose your preferred video quality
  4. Click "Download Video" and wait for processing
  5. Download your MP4 file when ready

Tip: Higher quality videos take longer to process and have larger file sizes. 720p is recommended for most uses.
