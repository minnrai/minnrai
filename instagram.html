How to use Instagram Downloader

For Posts & Reels:

  1. Open Instagram and find the content you want to download
  2. Tap the three dots (...) or the share icon (paper plane)
  3. Select "Copy Link"
  4. Paste the link here and click Download

For Stories:

  1. View the story you want to download
  2. Copy the story URL from your browser's address bar
  3. Paste it in the Story tab above and click Download
